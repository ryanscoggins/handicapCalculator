Scramble Pairing

Input player names and handicaps, then click "Create Teams" to generate 3 even matchups.

How team handicaps are calculated: All four players' handicaps are added together, then divided by 4 to get a combined scramble handicap for each pairing. Strokes awarded are based on the difference between the rounded-up team handicaps.
